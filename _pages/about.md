I'm a member of the technical staff at [AMI](https://amilabs.xyz/) working on physical world models. Previously, I was a research engineer at [FAIR](https://ai.facebook.com/research/), where I worked on fastMRI, neural compression, and V-JEPA 2. Prior to FAIR, I was a postdoctoral research fellow at the NYU radiology department, where I worked on MRI and x-ray CT reconstruction methods. My academic training was in medical signal and image processing at the University of Michigan under the supervision of Professors [Jeff Fessler](https://web.eecs.umich.edu/~fessler/) and [Doug Noll](https://nollresearch.engin.umich.edu). I got my bachelor's in biomedical engineering from Purdue University.
