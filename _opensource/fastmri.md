I am the lead maintainer of the [fastMRI GitHub Repository](https://github.com/facebookresearch/fastMRI). I began working on the repository in 2020. The repository is dedicated towards working with the [fastMRI Dataset](https://fastmri.med.nyu.edu) and reproducing papers of the fastMRI project, such as the [End-to-end Variational Network](https://arxiv.org/abs/2004.06688). Beginning in 2020 I refactored the repository and also helped our team at FAIR open-source models associated with the variational network paper and the [original fastMRI dataset paper](https://arxiv.org/abs/1811.08839).
