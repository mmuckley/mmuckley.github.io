I am a lead developer and maintainer of [CovidPrognosis](https://github.com/facebookresearch/CovidPrognosis), a repository for reproducing results in the paper, [COVID-19 Deterioration Prediction via Self-Supervised Representation Learning and Multi-Image Prediction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2101.04909). The repository includes scripts for pretraining models using the [Momentum Contrast](https://openaccess.thecvf.com/content_CVPR_2020/html/He_Momentum_Contrast_for_Unsupervised_Visual_Representation_Learning_CVPR_2020_paper.html) method and then fine-tuning the models on COVID patient data. The repository includes MoCo-pretrained models on public X-ray data.
